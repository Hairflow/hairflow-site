HairFlow AI

HairFlow AI is designed to help busy salons in Florida automate client communications and maximize appointments. Our platform sends automated appointment reminders, follow-up messages after visits, and even handles phone calls so customers can automatically book appointments over the phone. This allows you to focus on styling and growing your business while we handle the communication.

What We Do

HairFlow AI provides busy salons with a complete client communication solution. We send automated appointment reminders via text or email, follow up with messages to increase repeat bookings, automate your business calls so clients can make appointments over the phone, and notify you whenever a client makes an appointment. All of this is designed to save salon owners time, reduce no-shows, and increase revenue while keeping clients engaged.
